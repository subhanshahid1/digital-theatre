The Mad Hatter's Tea Party

1h 56m

or

Step into a fantastical world where being different is something to celebrate! The Mad Hatter’s Tea Party
brings Lewis Carroll’s classic to life with a hip-hop twist – it’s a high-energy, family-friendly show from
ZooNation, packed with dynamic dance and original music.

Set in a quirky institution, Ernest is the newcomer trying to help a group of characters who don’t quite fit
in – like Alice who struggles with her ever-changing size, and the Mad Hatter who is stuck in a
perpetual
tea-time frenzy. But in this colourful world, Ernest starts to wonder: what is normal anyway?
