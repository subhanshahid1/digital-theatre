Richard III

The Mad Hatter's Tea Party

The Mad Hatter's Tea Party

Step into a fantastical world where being different is something to celebrate! The Mad Hatter’s Tea Party brings Lewis Carroll’s classic to life with a hip-hop twist – it’s a high-energy, family-friendly show from ZooNation, packed with dynamic dance and original music.
Set in a quirky institution, Ernest is the newcomer trying to help a group of characters who don’t quite fit in – like Alice who struggles with her ever-changing size, and the Mad Hatter who is stuck in a perpetual tea-time frenzy. But in this colourful world, Ernest starts to wonder: what is normal anyway?

Much Ado About Nothing

Much Ado About Nothing

In one of Shakespeare's most loved comedies, Much Ado About Nothing, Beatrice and Benedick are adamant in their mutual dislike, while Claudio and Hero are deep in love, and the two stories take an unexpected course.
This production was captured by Digital Theatre live at Wyndham's Theatre in London’s West End. It played to sold-out houses and received wide critical acclaim. Directed by Josie Rourke, it starred David Tennant as Benedick and Catherine Tate as Beatrice.
